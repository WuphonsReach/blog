Level 12 is pretty important for a magician because we get the "Bind Affinity" spell which allows us to set our "home point" (the spot where we return to after we die or cast the "Gate" spell to return there voluntarily). This makes it much easier to travel long distances because you can set your bind point to a safe spot near your hunting grounds.
