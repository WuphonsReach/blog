Most trains in Blackburrow can be beaten down if everyone stays in the zone and stands their ground, but a pair of casters can't beat down a train of that size by themselves (we need melee classes to keep the gnolls busy while we burn them down). One more mid level character, or if 2 of the lower level characters had stood their ground we would have made it without zoning.
