Warchalking


Warchalking is a way that geeks communicate the presence of wireless access points to other geeks by scribling a symbol in chalk on walls or sidewalks. If I manage to see any symbols like this, I'll try to grab a picture of the spot.

Collaboratively creating a hobo-language for free wireless networking.
