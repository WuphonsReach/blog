--- layout: post title: MoHAA Vent Slug date: '2004-03-31T12:12:00.000-05:00' author: Wuphon's Reach tags: - '2004' modified_time: '2010-08-04T14:52:55.642-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3097974.post-108075317050087472 --- I'm guessing this is an "easter egg" in the Medal of Honor: Allied Assault game...

When escaping from Trondheim (where you destroy the Naxos prototype and sink one of the submarine U-boats), the last section of the mission starts with you in a vent duct. Well, you're not alone up there and a little ways ahead of you (and moving as fast as you move) is the elusive Trondheim Duct Vent Slug.







I wasn't able to find anyone else reporting this easter egg via google. OTOH, querying for +"medal of honor" +"allied assault" +"easter egg" escape vent duct came back with nothing but spammer sites for at least the first 15 pages worth of results.
