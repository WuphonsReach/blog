--- layout: post title: Call of Duty Key Settings date: '2004-06-26T23:50:00.000-04:00' author: Wuphon's Reach tags: - CallOfDuty modified_time: '2010-08-04T14:54:42.815-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3097974.post-108830848540615511 --- Needed to write these down so that I can setup the multi-player controls. Anything not mentioned is "unbound".

Look:
Free Look: Yes
Invert Mouse: No
Smooth Mouse: No
Mouse Sensitivity: (low, around 20%)

Move:
Forward: W
Backpedal: S
Move Left: A
Move Right: D
Lean Left: Q
Lean Right: E
Stand/Jump: [Space]
Stance Down: [Wheel Up]
Stance Up: [Wheel Down]
Toggle Crouch: [Ctrl]
Toggle Prone: [Z]

Shoot:
Attack: [Left Mouse]
Aim Down the Sight: [Right Mouse]
Melee Attack: [Shift] or [Middle Mouse]
Reload Weapon: R
Change Rate of Fire: M
Select First Weapon: 1
Select Second Weapon: 2
Select Pistold: 3
Select Grenade: 4

Save/Interact
Show Objectives/Scores: [Tab]
Use: F
Screenshot: [F12]
Quick Save: [F5]
Quick Load: [F9]
