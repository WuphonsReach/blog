--- layout: post title: Quiller Series - Timeline date: '2005-06-02T23:02:00.000-04:00' author: Wuphon's Reach tags: - '2005' modified_time: '2009-09-30T13:58:06.979-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3097974.post-111629941616032387 blogger_orig_url: http://wuphonsreach.blogspot.com/2005/06/quiller-series-timeline.shtml --- The Quiller series is one of those cold war delights. A book series about an undercover british agent who goes very deep undercover behind the iron/bamboo curtains. One turn of phrase used is "throwing the ferret down the hole to see what it can find out". He's often completely cut off from any backup and has to rely on his wits and ability to evade the opposition to survive and escape.

He's the anti-James Bond.
