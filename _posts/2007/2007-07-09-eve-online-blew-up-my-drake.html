--- layout: post title: EVE Online - Blew up my Drake date: '2007-07-09T09:00:00.000-04:00' author: Wuphon's Reach tags: - '2007' - EVEOnline modified_time: '2009-09-30T13:55:34.752-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3097974.post-7675625321417754891 --- So earlier this week, I started running level 3 missions in earnest. There's a definite step up in difficulty, loyalty points, reward ISK, and bounties between L2s and L3s. Things were going well, until I was working on a 10-part storyline mission. The last (10th) mission in the series involves a very nasty fleet battle with 6+ battleships, multiple battlecruisers and cruisers, and some frigates.

I had managed to pick off about half of the fleet (aggro a clump, fly directly away), but made a mistake when I went back to get another group. Instead of flying away towards a distant celestial object, I was aimed towards the nether regions of space. So when my Drake started getting pounded hard, I couldn't find anything to warp out to quickly enough to avoid destruction.

Between insurance for the hull, minus what I paid to insure a new hull and lost fittings, it was a 20-30M ISK mistake. On the other hand, I had made about 15-20M ISK on the storyline so far (killing those battleships with 1.2M ISK bounties helped). And the storyline mission resulted in a massive boost to my standings with the storyline agent. So on the whole, it was a minor setback not a major one.

But I'm definitely feeling a bit poorer this week.
