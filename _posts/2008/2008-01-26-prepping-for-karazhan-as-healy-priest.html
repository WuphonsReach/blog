But one of the goals of getting to L70 is also that I want to be ready for Karazhan. (Kara is a 10-man raid dungeon, considered the easiest of the post Burning Crusade dungeons. Our guild runs it about once a week.)

Some of the numbers that I've seen thrown around on the web work out to be:

Healing: +1000 (min) up to +1200 or +1400
MP5 (while casting): 120 up to 200
HP: 7000 hitpoints (buffed)
Mana: 9000 mana (buffed)

Which probably won't be terribly difficult to attain the lower end of those numbers with just green/blue equipment. The Primal Mooncloth set is going to go a long way towards shoring me up. But I still need to put together a wishlist of gear that I want to go hunting for.
