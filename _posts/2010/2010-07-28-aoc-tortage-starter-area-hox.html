--- layout: default title: 'AoC: Tortage starter area (HoX)' date: '2010-07-28T15:26:00.002-04:00' author: Wuphon's Reach tags: - AgeOfConan modified_time: '2010-07-28T15:32:52.316-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3097974.post-4595391887820729644 --- A few short clips showing the character creation process and some basic questing in the new player city of Tortage. Players spend the first 16-20 levels in and around Tortage as they learn their classes and work through the first part of their "destiny" quest chain. This is probably one of the things that Age of Conan got right, with the first 5 levels being spent in a solo instance where you slowly gain abilities and a tiny bit of gear. Basically, it's a 30-40 minute tutorial as you level from 1-5, followed by the city of Tortage which introduces you to other players.

The character creation process is fairly straightforward, other then it being too easy to create your character on a server that you weren't expecting.



The next two clips are the Tortage starting city, showing a young Herald of Xotli (HoX) going around and getting quests from NPCs. There's also a bit of HoX combat, which is a mix of melee/magic.
