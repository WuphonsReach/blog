Things you'll want to keep in mind:
  • There's a few different ways to approach things in this segment. So save right at the start and try again if you're not content with the outcome.
  • The [Tab] key will bring up the Pipboy, pressing and holding [Tab] will turn the Pipboy light on and off. The light provides a small (few meters at the most) bubble of light around you, useful for exploring dark environments.
  • Some conversations are triggered by proximity. As soon as you get close to the person, your camera will swing around and you'll be forcibly engaged in conversation.
  • I generally remap the "Use" key to [F] instead of the default of [E].
  • The [R] key will ready your weapon (or fists), reload it, or if held down it will put the weapon (or fists) away.
  • When fighting unarmed, [Left-Click] to throw a pair of punches and [Right-Click] to bring up your fists and block.
  • If you're looking at your inventory in the Pipboy, [Left-Click] will use/apply the item (such as a Stimpak or reading a skillbook like the comic book you were given). [Right-Click] will drop the item to the ground at your feet (if it can be dropped).
