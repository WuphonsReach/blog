--- layout: default title: 'FO3: MMM Gargantuan, South of Paradise Falls' date: '2010-08-11T03:23:00.001-04:00' author: Wuphon's Reach tags: - Fallout3 modified_time: '2010-08-12T15:45:54.682-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3097974.post-2527289221131762613 --- One of the mods that I use is called MMM (Martigen's Mutant Mod). If you thought Super Mutant Behemoths were large and a PITA, well the mod adds Gargantuans which are even larger and meaner.

If you are very very unlucky, you can even discover them out in the middle of nowhere. At first, I didn't notice the big orange thing as it was obscured by the monorail track support. It wasn't until I started my attack on the Super Mutant Brutes that I realized what it was.

For my 3rd attempt - I simply ran far far away without engaging that group.
