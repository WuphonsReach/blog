This is my first raiding in over a year, so I'm very rusty. My user interface also leaves a bit to be desired and I really think I need to remap one or two spells to different keys.

Lower Spire - Entrance Hall Trash



Lower Spire - Lord Marrowgar



Lower Spire - Gunship Battle
The gunship battle fight. Xav fell off the side of the ship at about the 3:15 mark and got teleported back up. I also misunderstood the call to change ships, and though the "main" ship was the Horde's ship and not the Alliance's ship. So you'll see that I jump over and then jump back.



Lower Spire - Deathbringer Saurfang
This fight is the last of the "Lower Spire" (first area) of ICC. I forgot to turn on the FRAPS recorder at the start, so I missed out on the NPC dialog.



Plagueworks - Stinky
Stinky is a "miniboss" that patrols the hallways leading up to the first two major bosses in the Plagueworks portion of ICC. There's also another "miniboss" named "Precious" that patrols the other hallway.



Plagueworks - Rotface

A fairly easy boss, except I didn't really know what I was doing. The "professor" voice in the background is the final boss of the wing.



Plagueworks - Festergut
Of the two "patchwork" type bosses at the start of Plagueworks, Festergut is considered to be the more difficult of the two. It took us four total attempts to pull this off with the pickup raid.
