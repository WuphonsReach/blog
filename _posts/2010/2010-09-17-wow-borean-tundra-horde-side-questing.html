--- layout: post title: 'WoW: Borean Tundra (horde-side questing)' date: '2010-09-17T19:04:00.000-04:00' author: Wuphon's Reach category: WorldOfWarcraft tags: - WorldOfWarcraft modified_time: '2010-09-20T07:05:57.052-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3097974.post-2992729589123160966 --- Traveling from Vengeance Landing in Howling Fjord, back to Tirisfal Glades via the zepplin. Then over to Durotar via the cross-ocean zepplin before heading north again to Warsong Hold in Borean Tundra. This is usually faster then taking the walrus people's turtle boats if you are going west (BT) to east (HF). And a lot less expensive then the flight from Warsong Hold to Vengeance Landing.



One of the early quests on Horde-faction is a vehicle quest called "Plains of Nasam" where you drive a Horde Siege Tank (called a Destroyer in Wintergrasp) to take on large numbers of enemy NPCs.

Kel'Thuzad makes a bit of a cameo in Borean Tundra.
