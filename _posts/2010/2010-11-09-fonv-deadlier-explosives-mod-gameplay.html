It ramps up the damage on explosives so that they are a lot more useful and deadly.

40mm Incendiary

Here's what the 40mm Incendiary grenades are capable of if they are made slightly more powerful (using the "Deadlier Explosives New Vegas" mod). I managed to take on the exterior of McCarren, a major NCR stronghold, and mostly survive although I lost 2 companions. Makes me almost think that the mod boosted the effect too much, but it works.



25mm Incendiary Grenades

These are added by my mod for Fallout New Vegas. They're fine against unarmored foes, but really not suitable for use against Deathclaws (find something bigger).



The "Fat Man" (hunting Deathclaws)

The default mini-nuke explosion has been severely nerfed compared to Fallout 3, this mod attempts to restore it to its former glory. Especially since the ammo is extremely expensive and rare. It is now a properly fearsome weapon with a big splash radius. The ammo is still expensive...
