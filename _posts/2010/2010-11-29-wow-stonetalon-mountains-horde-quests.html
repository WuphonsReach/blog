There are a lot more "bread-crumb" type quests now that lead you from one area to another after you finish up the quests in a particular location.

"We're Here to Do One Thing, Maybe Two..." is the quest that takes you from Silverwind Refuge in Ashenvale down to The Fold in Stonetalon Mountains. Blizzard has really taken a shine to vehicles since the Wrath of the Lich King expansion and you will find a lot more quests using or centered around vehicles in the old-world zones now. Unfortunately, the animation isn't always quite there yet and needs to be cleaned up a bit.

One of the new quests in World of Warcraft's Cataclysm is "Dream of a Better Tomorrow" where you have to go collect parts for a war machine on the horde-side. Three of the parts are easy to get to, but the fourth "BD-816 War Apparatus" may be a bit confusing to find.

To get this, you have to swing around the north side of the lake and find the narrow path leading up to the giant water wheel in the sky.



This is "Mr. D's Wild Ride" where you put that war machine to good use.

In exchange for an ice-cream cone.
