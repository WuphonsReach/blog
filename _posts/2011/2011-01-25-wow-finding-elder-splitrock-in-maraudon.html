--- layout: default title: 'WoW: Finding Elder Splitrock in Maraudon (Lunar Festival)' date: '2011-01-25T13:10:00.002-05:00' author: Wuphon's Reach tags: - WorldOfWarcraft modified_time: '2011-01-25T13:19:08.966-05:00' thumbnail: https://img.youtube.com/vi/IV0zBJMmRQ4/default.jpg blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3097974.post-8746091858521132496 --- Shows how to find Elder Splitrock down in the Maraudon dungeon in World of Warcraft during the Lunar Festival. The Maraudon dungeon can be a bit tricky to navigate.

The basic instructions are:
- Enter the entrance in Desolace (Kalimdor continent).
- Look for the wooden cage, followed by the two flights of stairs (0:52).
- When you see purple crystals to the left *and* orange to the right, go straight forward into the doorway guarded by a pair of centaur statues.
- This passage leads to the portal that takes you to "Earth Song Falls" in Maraudon
- Jump down into the pool below (01:45)
- Go straight into the cave south of the pool, to the back corner of the cave (02:40)
- Go past the dinos until you reach an upward ramp (03:30), left takes you to the stone giants, the right takes you where you want to go.
- You'll see that from 03:30 to 05:00, I went left (the wrong way) into the giants, then circled back around to the ramp
- Deep Borer mobs will be the first things you see on the right after the T-intersection (05:15)
- Go to the right and down the multiple ramps (05:25)
- You will enter "Zaeter's Grave" where you go left to the Elder (06:30)
- Cross the stone bridge (06:45)
- Past even more Deep Borers (07:00)
- You will then see Elder Splitrock (07:15)

The easiest way back out of that part of Mara is hearth or portal.
