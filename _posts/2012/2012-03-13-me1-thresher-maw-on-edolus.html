Since I've never seen one before, it took me a few tries to get it killed. I even got double-XP for killing it for some reason.



Note that if you can get away from the Thresher Maw, such as up on a hill, you can press the [F] key on the PC to repair the Mako. It takes 20 seconds or so to fully repair and uses up a bunch of omni-gel.
