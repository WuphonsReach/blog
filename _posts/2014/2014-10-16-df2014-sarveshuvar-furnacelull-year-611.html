I now have about 150 bedrooms for the dwarves, each is 2x2, engraved, with a bed and cabinet along with doors in the corners.  They are all set for first-come first-serve.  I plan on finishing out the fourth layer this year, bringing us to 196 total bedrooms.


Furnacelull is now a city and created wealth is about 6.5 million.  The drink stocks are at 1200, which is roughly 10 drinks per dwarf.


The cooks have fallen behind on turning eggs into fancy lavish meals, so we have 2500 meat, 285 fish, 9300 eggs and 15 cheese.  The number of prepared meals is up to 12,800.  Hopefully I can drop that number to about 6000 after the caravan arrives in the fall.

Ammo stocks are so-so.  Ideally I want about 100 metal bolts per marksdwarf.  So with five squads of ten dwarves, I'd want about 5000 metal bolts.  I only have about 1500 metal bolts.  Before I bulk up the marksdwarf militia squads, I need to bulk up my metal ammo supplies.  I have 165 iron, 127 copper, 147 steel, and about 75 bronze bars.  If I convert the 127 copper and 75 bronze into bolts that gives me about 5000.

I'll produce those using a novice weaponsmith.  Turning off my two near-legendary weaponsmiths from producing any weapons for the duration.

The (3) "pillars" squads now all have masterwork steel battle axes.  Future recruits will probably ask for other things.  I'll start production of iron armor and they'll be assigned "metal" armor in a few months.  Since I want high-quality armor right off the bat, I'll let my near-legendary armorer make those pieces.

The Archery range only has room for (4) targets.  I will be constructing a second one in the SE corner.


Note the open spaces between the firing lanes.  The misses are collected on the floor below.
