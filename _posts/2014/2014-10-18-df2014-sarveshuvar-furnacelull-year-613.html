I'm going to form four new crossbow equipped militia squads around these families.  Legendary crafters will be moved into those four squads who will stay on reserve duty most of the year except for two months in the late winter.

This also means realignment of who belongs to which squad.

(4) Golden squads - These are the legendaries and mothers of the fortress and train only once per year in the winter, for two months.  They are equipped with leather armor, trousers, cloaks and hoods but with metal helms / gauntlets / high boots.  They get iron crossbows and are allowed to use metal bolts for training.

(5) Silvery squads - These are the females of the fort (but not mothers).  Same armor as the goldens but get assigned to slightly more hazardous duties like refuse / wood hauling.

(3) Pillars squads - The males of the fort (but not fathers or legendaries).  Leather tunic, metal mail shirt, metal breastplate, leather trousers with metal greaves, metal high boot, metal gauntlet, metal helm, two leather hoods and two leather cloaks.  The weapon is a steel melee wapon of their choice.

At the moment, there are now 105 dwarves under arms.  It will likely take until late spring for all of the gear / equipment to be shuffled around.  I'm running DFHack "cleanowned scattered" about every two weeks to clean up as dwarves drop their old gear in favor of their new uniforms.
