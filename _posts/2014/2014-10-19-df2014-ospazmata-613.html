The other two dwarven civs are on the ropes.  So I'm going to create a pair of 3x3 embarks, get them up to 150 dwarves, get the king there, setup steel, and then retire them.

Looking around in the LNP that I downloaded, I found that it comes with Isoworld 2.1.  Here is the island area where Furnacelull was settled.  You can see that it is completely separated from the mainland.


If I switch to the "Trade" view, I can figure out where Furnacelull is at.


The "Elevation" view is also useful.  The 'g' key turns the grid/on off.


Looking at the map along the edges, there is possibly another pair of isolated islands in the very N/NE part of the map along the north edge.  Probably frozen since it is close to the North Pole.


And another one in the very SW corner.  Probably frozen since it is close to the South Pole.


So it looks like I'll settle the next Fortress back on the western island.  That seems to be the best spot in this world.
